Hamwise Studios ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our games.

Information We Collect

Our games may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or phone number unless you contact us directly via email.

Advertising

Our games may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners, including Google AdMob and mediation partners such as LevelPlay (ironSource).

These partners may collect and use data (including advertising identifiers and device information) to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how players use our games in order to improve gameplay and stability. These services may collect anonymous usage data.

Children's Privacy

Our games are not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

Data Security
