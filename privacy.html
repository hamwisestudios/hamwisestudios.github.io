Dolphams – Privacy Policy

Hamwise Studios

Last updated: 4th November, 2022

About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Dolphams, developed by Hamwise Studios, collects and uses your information when you use the game.

What Information Is Collected?

The amount of personal information collected is kept to a minimum and is only used to provide essential game features.

The following information may be collected:

  • Email Address (via Google Play Games authentication)
  • Device information (such as device type and operating system)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple Identifier for Advertisers)
  • Anonymous gameplay and usage data

Purpose of Collected Information

Email Address

Your email address is used solely to allow you to save personal game data online through Google Play Games services. This allows you to maintain progress, purchases, and unlocks across devices when authenticated through Google Play Games.

If you uninstall the game or switch devices, your progress can be restored when signing in again.

Advertising

Dolphams may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks, including Google AdMob and mediation partners such as LevelPlay (ironSource).

These services may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to:

  • Provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • Measure advertising performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You may limit ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to:

  • Provide services on our behalf
  • Assist in analyzing how the game is used
  • Provide advertising services

These third parties may have access to limited information solely for performing their assigned tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

Deleting Your Data

If you would like your saved data deleted, you may:

  • Email: dolphamsgame@gmail.com
  • Disable the in-game setting named “Save Online”. Unticking this box will prevent further data uploads once removed.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, you may request deletion using the contact information above.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
