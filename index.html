Available Games

Can You Digit

A simple maths game where you solve as many problems as you can within 60 seconds. Difficulty can be scaled so that you can challenge yourself no matter what your skill level is.

Rotatoes

Use joysticks to rotate rings to let potatoes through to the middle. If a potato hits a ring, you lose. Each ring only needs to consider potatoes of the same colour and you can play with 1, 2 or 3 rings.

Counting On Ewe

Count sheep that jump across the screen. Be careful to only count the colours of sheep that are listed at the top of the screen. Three different difficulties to always give you a challenge.

Otter My Way!

Direct traffic with otters to help them reach their destinations. If too many otters get caught in traffic then you lose, so try to keep them moving as much as possible.

Ballpoint Den

Launch pens at balls to destroy them. When a pen reaches its highest point and begins to fall back down, it will turn into an extra ball that you need to destroy. If a ball reaches the bottom you lose.
